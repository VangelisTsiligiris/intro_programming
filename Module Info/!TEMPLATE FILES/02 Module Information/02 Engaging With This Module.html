Engaging With This Module 

To fully benefit from the SOFT40161: Introduction to Computer Programming module, students are expected to actively engage with the content and participate in various activities. To get started, familiarize yourself with the module’s online learning platform, where you will find essential resources, lecture materials, lab assignments, and additional readings.

Engagement Expectations:

  • Participation in Activities: Attend all lectures and lab sessions, as these will include hands-on exercises, discussions, and collaborative projects. Active participation is crucial for understanding programming concepts and applying them effectively.
  • Routine Communication: Use email and Microsoft Teams to communicate regularly with peers, the module leader and the support tutor. Forming study groups or discussion threads on Teams can enhance your learning experience and provide additional support.

As your module leader, I will facilitate discussions, provide guidance during lab sessions, and be available for queries via email and Teams. I anticipate fostering a supportive learning environment where you feel comfortable sharing your challenges and successes.

Technology Requirements: Students are expected to have access to a computer with a stable internet connection and the ability to install Python and necessary libraries. Basic digital literacy skills, such as navigating software, managing files, and using online communication tools, are essential for success in this module.

For those needing extra support, resources and development sessions on Python installation, IDE usage, and programming fundamentals will be provided. Links to additional support resources, tutorials, and help sessions will be available on the module’s online platform.

By engaging actively with this module and utilizing the available resources, you will be well-equipped to develop the programming skills necessary for success

.
